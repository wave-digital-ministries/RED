Be Biblically correct, not politically

We are called to not be of this world.

We are called in several passages of the Bible not to "conform to this world."

We are also informed by scripture that when we are not of this world, we will not be liked by those who are.

What does "being of this world" really mean?

"Being of this world" means living an earthly life style that is accepted by the earthly. However, what it does NOT mean is acting as though one is above others. Romans 3:23, "for all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." We need to do our best to live according to God's Word in all aspects of our lives.
