ENVIRONMENTALISM AND THE BIBLE

We are called to be stewards of God's creation.

"Then God said, 'Let us make mankind in our image, in our likeness, so that they may rule over the fish in the sea and the birds in the sky, over the livestock and all the wild animals, and over all the creatures that move along the ground.'”

Genesis 1:26 NIV

What does this mean?

This means that we are to be stewards of God's Creation and take care of it and utilize His creation. We can use resources God created while also respecting and maintaining God's creation. This may look like...

Recycling; Supporting reforestation and lumber projects; reducing waste; etc
